Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect energy usage data (Voltage, Fuel Levels) solely to provide analytics and outage predictions. We do not share this data with third parties.

2. Location Services

GridPilot uses your location only to map outage trends in your area (e.g., Victoria Island, Lagos). We do not track your real-time movements.

3. Data Security

All data sent from your GridPilot Hardware Node is encrypted using standard SSL protocols to ensure your home security is not compromised.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about your data, please contact support@gridpilot.ng.
